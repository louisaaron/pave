During Spring 2022, the PAVE team is partnering with Princeton Electric Speedboating to build a boat that will race in the Promoting Electric Propulsion for Small Craft competition. The boat will operate autonomously using GPS and computer vision systems and will have no driver onboard. The PEP competition takes place in May 2022 in Occoquan, VA.
